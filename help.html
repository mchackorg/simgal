Album Help

In the album index, click on the small images (thumbnails) to show a preview page with a slightly larger image.

In the preview page there are three images: two small thumbnails and a larger preview image of the image you clicked on.

Use the two small images to go the previous (left) or next (right) image in the album. Click on the larger image to see the image at its original size.

You can also use accesskeys instead of clicking with the mouse. Accesskeys are combination of keys on your keyboard. For example, in Firefox under Windows or Unix, you press Alt + Shift + the accesskey at the same time.

This gallery uses these accesskeys in the preview page:

  • U goes up to the album index.
  • P goes to the previous image in the album.
  • N goes to next image in the album.
  • Z goes to the full size of the image (zoom).
